Parallelizing Neural Network Hyperparameter Tuning

02/2023 – 05/2023 Completed

Developed an innovative approach combining openMP multi-threading and Asynchronous Successive Halving to optimize hyperparameter search for neural networks, achieving significant performance improvements.

Key Results

  • Devised approach combining openMP multi-threading and Asynchronous Successive Halving for optimal hyperparameter configurations
  • Achieved 3.5x speedup over sequential baseline using single compute node
  • Maintained model flexibility while significantly reducing training time
